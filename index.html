Correlation Between Poverty and Lack of Healthcare Coverage

The chart explores the relationship between the proportion of individuals living in poverty in each state and the proportion of individuals without healthcare coverage in that state. Clicking on the data points shows the two parameters for the respective data point.

Observations

The proportion of persons without healthcare coverage is positively correlated with the proportion of persons living in poverty. The calculated correlation between poverty and lack of Healthcare coverage is 0.524. Puerto Rico is an extreme outlier that reduces the correlation to 0.106. Puerto Rico has therfore been ommitted from the chart.
